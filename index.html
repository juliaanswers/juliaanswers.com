How it works

No setup headaches. Julia plugs into your business phone number and starts answering calls right away.

A customer calls

Julia picks up, greets them by name if they've called before, and asks how she can help — just like a real person.

She handles it

Check what's in stock, place an order, schedule a pickup time — Julia takes care of it in the conversation, using real tools connected to your business.

You get a text

After the call, you get an SMS summary with what happened, what was ordered, and anything that needs your attention.
