How it works

1.

A customer calls

Julia picks up, greets them by name if they've called before, and asks how she can help.

2.

She handles it

Check what's in stock, place an order, schedule a pickup time — Julia takes care of it in the conversation.

3.

You get a text

After the call, you get an SMS summary with what happened, what was ordered, and anything that needs your attention.
